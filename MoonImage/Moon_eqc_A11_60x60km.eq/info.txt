LRO Narrow Angle Camera, Mosaic Apollo 11
Layer ID : A11_60x60km.eq
bbox : 22.4806483,-0.3193414,24.459338,1.6593483
WMTS Endpoint : https://trek.nasa.gov/tiles/Moon/EQ/A11_60x60km.eq
Abstract : This is a Lunar Reconnaisance Orbiter (LRO) Narrow Angle Camera (NAC) mosaic centered at the Apollo 11 landing site with an extent of 60x60km. Each NAC image was processed in ISIS3 at a resolution of 50cm pixel, color balanced, and synthesized into a single output.
这是一个月球侦察轨道飞行器（LRO）窄角相机（NAC）马赛克，以阿波罗11号着陆点为中心，范围为60x60公里。每个 NAC 图像都在 ISIS3 中以 50 厘米像素的分辨率进行处理，色彩平衡并合成为单个输出。
